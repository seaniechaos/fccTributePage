Ludwig von Mises

The Last Knight of Liberalism

Here's a partial timeline of von Mises's life:

  • 1881 - September 29. Born in Lemberg, Austria. Father, Arthur Edler von Mises. Mother, Adele (Landau) von Mises. Brother, Richard von Mises (1883–1953).
  • 1892-2000 - Attended Akademische Gymnasium, Vienna.
  • 1900 - Entered University of Vienna to study for a degree in law.
  • 1906 - February 20. Received doctor of jurisprudence degree (literally, doctor of both, canon and roman, laws).
  • 1909-1934 - Economic councillor of the Austrian Chamber of Commerce (Kammer für Handel, Gewerte und Industrie). Mises∗ chamber appraised legislation and advised the government on public policy.
  • 1934-1940 - Accepted professorship at the Graduate Institute of International Studies (Institut Universitaire de Hautes Etudes Internationales) In Geneva, Switzerland.
  • 1900-1944 - Guest of the National Bureau of Economic Research in New York.
  • 1945-1969 - Appointed visiting professor at Graduate School of Business Administration, New York University.
  • 1946-1973 - Advisor to Foundation for Economic Education, Inc., Irvington-on-Hudson, New York.
  • 1973 - October 10. Died at 92 years of age.
